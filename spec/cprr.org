* Overview

CPRR = Conjecture-Prediction-Result-Refutation.
Each conjecture has an explicit refutation condition and refinement path.

* Conjecture Table

| C-id | Conjecture                                                | Refutation condition                                            | Refinement on refutation                                       |
|------+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------|
| C-1  | Every opted-in service propagates ~traceparent~ on egress | Service emits outbound call without ~traceparent~ in CI fixture | Middleware regression; service removed from opted-in list until fixed |

* Notes

** C-5: Meta-conjecture

C-5 is the meta-conjecture that the harness catches everything the spec claims.
The harness can never fully prove this about itself.

Mitigation: every production violation found post-merge MUST add a regression
property to the harness. The conjecture becomes "harness coverage is monotonically
increasing in violation history."

** C-7: Cross-team boundary

C-7 sits at the trace-spine/otel-local boundary. Naming it explicitly avoids
the failure mode where each team assumes the other owns it.

Owner: trace-spine names the conjecture; otel-local owns the reconciliation policy.
Escalation path: cross-team incident when orphan spans persist post-heal.
